Terms and Policies of Shri Manokamneshwar Mahadev Mandir

1. Devotee Conduct:

- All devotees are expected to conduct themselves with utmost respect and reverence within the premises of Shri Manokamneshwar Mahadev Mandir. - Loud noises, disruptive behavior, or any form of disrespect towards the sanctity of the temple and its surroundings will not be tolerated.

2. Dress Code:

- Devotees are requested to dress modestly and appropriately, adhering to traditional norms while visiting the temple. - Revealing attire and clothing with inappropriate slogans or graphics are not permitted within the temple premises.

3. Offerings and Donations:

- Devotees are encouraged to make offerings and donations towards the maintenance and welfare of the temple. - All offerings must be made with pure intentions and respect towards Lord Shiva and the temple.

4. Photography and Videography:

- Photography and videography within the temple premises are allowed only in designated areas. - Prior permission must be obtained from the temple authorities for any professional or commercial photography.

5. Mobile Phones and Electronic Devices:

- Devotees are requested to maintain silence and refrain from using mobile phones or electronic devices in the main prayer hall. - All electronic devices must be switched off or set to silent mode during the visit to the temple.

6. Personal Belongings:

- The temple management is not responsible for any loss or damage to personal belongings of devotees. - Visitors are advised not to leave valuables unattended within the temple premises.

7. Prasad Distribution:

- Prasad distribution is conducted with the blessings of Lord Shiva and should be accepted with gratitude. - Devotees are requested to consume prasad within the designated areas and maintain cleanliness.

8. Children and Pets:

- Children must be accompanied by adults and supervised at all times during their visit to the temple. - Pets are not allowed within the temple premises, except for service animals.

9. Respect for Sacred Objects:

- Devotees are urged to handle sacred objects, idols, and artifacts within the temple with care and reverence. - Touching or tampering with any sacred objects without permission is strictly prohibited.

10. Compliance with Temple Rules:

- All visitors are expected to comply with the rules and regulations set forth by the temple authorities. - Failure to adhere to the rules may result in refusal of entry or expulsion from the temple premises.

By visiting Shri Manokamneshwar Mahadev Mandir, devotees agree to abide by the aforementioned terms and policies. The temple management reserves the right to modify or update these terms and policies as deemed necessary for the well-being and sanctity of the temple.
